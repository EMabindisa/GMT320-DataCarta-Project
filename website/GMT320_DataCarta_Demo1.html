    Our Focus

    3D Mapping and Rooftop Gardens

    At DataCarta, our focus is on leveraging Geographic Information System (GIS) technology to display a 3D map of the University of Pretoria and introduce rooftop fruit and vegetable gardens. Our mission is to address UN Sustainable Development Goal 2 by promoting sustainable food production and distribution.

    Community Impact

    We are dedicated to making a positive impact on the community through the introduction of rooftop gardens and sustainable food distribution. By integrating technology and agriculture, we aim to enhance food security and environmental sustainability.
